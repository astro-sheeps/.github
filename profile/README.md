## 🐑 About AstroSheeps

AstroSheeps explores the intersection of **AI**, **robotics**, **smart telescopes**, and **playful mission-control design**.

The project started with a small rover experiment, but quickly grew into a broader tiny space-program idea: rovers explore the ground, telescopes explore the sky, and AI helps connect the mission.

AstroSheeps is not a professional space agency.  
It is a small Earth-based mission lab for curious explorers, balcony astronomers, robot builders, and people who like their engineering with a bit of sheep energy.

---

## 🚀 Mission

Our mission is to make small technical experiments feel like real exploration programs.

We build tools, websites, logs, planners, visual systems, and prototypes that help turn hobby-scale robotics and astronomy into structured missions:

- choose a target;
- plan the session;
- run the experiment;
- log the result;
- learn from failure;
- celebrate the tiny victory.

Sometimes the rover gets lost.  
Sometimes the clouds win.  
The mission continues anyway.

## 🔭 Divisions

### Rover Division

AI-assisted rover experiments, autonomous navigation, obstacle avoidance, sensors, cameras, simulation, and mission-planning workflows.

**Focus:** robotics, autonomy, perception, AI agents, tiny rover missions.

---

### Observatory Division

Robotic balcony astronomy with smart telescopes, Messier observing, target planning, planisphere tools, logs, observing cards, and mission-style sky exploration.

**Focus:** Seestar-style observing, Messier objects, observing workflows, sky planning, mission logs.

---

### Mission Control

The software layer that connects the mission: dashboards, websites, reports, AI assistants, visual systems, documentation, and project interfaces.

**Focus:** web tools, AI-assisted planning, logs, reports, GitHub Pages, visual storytelling.
